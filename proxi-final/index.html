Proxi Support

Proxi is a wearable ultra-wideband (UWB) badge paired with a mobile app that helps users locate friends at crowded events using a real-time “hot-cold” interface. Unlike GPS-based tools, Proxi uses precise UWB ranging to deliver accuracy within 20 cm, even indoors or in dense festival environments without WiFi. Designed for concerts, college campuses, and conferences, Proxi lets users connect with friends — and even discover new ones — without the battery drain, or delay and inaccuracy of traditional location sharing.
